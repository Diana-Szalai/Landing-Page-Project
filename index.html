PitStop
  • What we do

PitStop is a comunity kitchen
that redistributes food through a
pay-what-you-can-eat dinner service.

Learn more about us

WHAT WE DO

Minimize food waste through food rescue, redistribution, and repurposing

Help and reintegrate marginalized communities through our dinner services and job training

Provide a platform for those who want to give back to society but are not sure
